Nigerian Customs Service
Excise Portal

Excise Duty

A percentage levied on manufacture, sale or use of locally produced goods such as alcoholic drinks, tobacco,soap and detergents, telephone recharge cards and vouchers, toilet papers and facial tisues e.t.c.

Excise Regulations

Manufacturers of excisable products are required to apply for provisional /Final Approval to Nigeria Customs Service through the supervising Area Command
